About

SDG 3: Health & Well-being

Ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all at all ages is essential to sustainable development. Significant strides have been made in increasing life expectancy and reducing some of the common killers associated with child and maternal mortality. Major progress has been made on increasing access to clean water and sanitation, reducing malaria, tuberculosis, polio and the spread of HIV/AIDS. However, many more efforts are needed to fully eradicate a wide range of diseases and address many different persistent and emerging health issues.

GOALS

GOALS

  • Improve healthcare systems globally and ensure universal access to medical services.
  • Reduce child mortality and increase maternal healthcare support.
  • Combat infectious diseases such as malaria, tuberculosis.
  • Strengthen mental health awareness and access to psychological support.

VISION

VISION

  • A world where everyone enjoys good health & well-being.
  • Empower individuals to take control of their health.
  • Reduce preventable diseases & health inequalities.
  • Build stronger, healthier communities globally.
  • Make mental and physical well-being a universal priority.

MISSION

MISSION

  • Ensure universal healthcare access for all.
  • Educate communities on disease prevention & healthy living.
  • Promote mental health awareness & support.
  • Advocate for sustainable healthcare solutions.
  • Support medical research & innovation
